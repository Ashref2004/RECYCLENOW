Mechanical Recycling

Mechanical recycling recovers waste materials through physical processes like shredding, washing, and melting without chemical changes.

85% Energy Saved
60% Less Pollution

Chemical Recycling

Chemical recycling breaks down polymers into original monomers through processes like pyrolysis and depolymerization.

  • Processes contaminated plastics
  • Creates high-quality outputs
  • Complements mechanical recycling

Biological Recycling

Biological recycling uses natural processes like composting and anaerobic digestion to break down organic waste.

Separate organic waste from other materials

Decomposition

Microorganisms break down the material

Product
